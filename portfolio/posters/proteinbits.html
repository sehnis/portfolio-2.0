Description:
ProteinBits is a health food startup vreated by an Entrepreneurship student at the University of Michigan. Michigan Advertising and Marketing worked with this startup as a client during the Fall 2016 and Winter 2017 semesters, and through this partnership I was able to create these promotional materials for use in their entry to the Michigan Business challenge. These designs built off of the existing logos and motifs used by the brand, while maintaining a modern aesthetic. ProteinBits would go on to win a $10,000 prize from this competition, and has continued to expand the reach and sale of their product.
