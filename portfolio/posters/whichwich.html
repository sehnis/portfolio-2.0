Which Wich, although a fantastic sandwich restaurant, is not one of the local companies working with Michigan Advertising and Marketing. However, as a group event to improve our print design skills, the design team all chose a local business to make a poster concept for. I produced this example, which represents some of my most recent work and progress in developing a simple yet straightforward aesthetic. As such, this poster was in no way presented to the actual business, and the rights to the logo belong respectively to Which Wich.
