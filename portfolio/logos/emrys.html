Name:
Emrys


Industry:
SaaS Startup


Description:
Emrys is a service using cloud infrastructure that combines a number of open-source languages and tools for data science applications. Its name comes from one of the names associated with the mythical figure Merlin, and as such the team wanted a logo that evoked a sense of magic without appealing too strongly to any single demographic.

Instead of opting for a direct representation of a wizard or witch, I decided to create the logo based on potions; they appealed to the desired sense of magic, while maintaining implications with the vast potential for creations. This proposal would not be selected, but the proposal was the first of its kind in the competition, which resulted in a similarly potion-based logo for the company.
