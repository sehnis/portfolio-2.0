Name:
ChestnutAR


Industry:
AR / VR Development


Description:
ChestnutAR is the expansion of an existing communications company into the field of Augmented Reality. Their line of existing and potential products is wide-ranging, all revolving around the central concept of improving how to better visualize information.

For this logo proposal, I combined the form of a chestnut viewed from below with an orientation that implied expansion into 3D space. It stays simple, while staying true to its namesake and evoking a proper feel for a modern tech company.
