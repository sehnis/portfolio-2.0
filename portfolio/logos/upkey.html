Sam Ehnis-Clark
Name:
Upkey


Industry:
Recruiting / Networking


Description:
Upkey is an incubator that aims to connect college students with companies; namely, it aims to allow students from underrepresented circumstances to gain the advising and resources that they need to best reach their potential. To appeal to this college demographic, they looked for a logo that was modern and inventive while staying youthful.

My proposal combined two motifs that the team listed in their design brief, in order to illustrate their notion of helping talented students. By combining a bird with a jetpack, this logo pushed the idea that the students in the program are already talented and capable, yet can be given that extra bit of support and ability through the help of the program. The lighthearted representation of this metaphor, however, kept the appeal relatively approachable. Unfortunately, the contest associated with this rebranding was cancelled before completion, despite the company still continuing with their own establishment and brand identity.
