Name:
Jibber


Industry:
IoT Application


Description:
Jibber is an Internet of Things-oriented application that grants users social media functionality similar to Twitter through their digital assistants like Alexa and Google Home. Through this platform, online communication is even more accessable and ubiquitous than on mobile devices; friends and content creators alike are able to speak naturally and achieve the same digital reach as if they were typing their thoughts on a computer.

Playing to this notion of an ever-present source of friendly communication, I based this proposal off of the notion of a pet bird. This icon combines the letter J of Jibber with an abstract bird figure, with a cheerful color scheme that further emphasizes the welcoming aspect of the app. Though this motif was eventually rejected in favor of squirrel iconography, this version went through several iterations to best construct its symbolism.
