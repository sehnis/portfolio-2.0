Description:
threesixty was a VR development and research studio, which aimed to support developers through assisting them in finding real people to test and give feedback to their products. The logo proposal combines the general shape of an eye with an abstract representation of 360: the outer circle is divided 3 times, the middle one is divided 6 times, and the inner one 0 times.
