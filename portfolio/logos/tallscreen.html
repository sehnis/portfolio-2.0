Name:
Tallscreen


Industry:
Mobile / Web Application


Description:
Tallscreen is a mobile video company, that produces software able to optimize advertisements for mobile phones. Whereas traditional, horizontally-filmed videos are better suited for computers, vertical videos fit ideally into the screens of smartphones; this notion is what the company hoped to build off of.

They requested a logo design that was sophisticated and relatively abstract, while staying approachable. Though the logo contest would be cancelled before its completion, my proposal used both an abstract letter mark and a motif of playing videos to create a memorable yet reasonably complex logo.
