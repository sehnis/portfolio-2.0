Description:
PLVS is a sports jewelry company, whose goal is to offer bracelets and other jewelry designed with and for their favorite sports teams. This proposal aimed to emphasize the use of a V instead of a U in PLVS, as well as subtly hint at a plus sign using the whitespace breaking the central figure.
